##  Project Overview

As a newly selected **Student Digital Ambassador**, my role is to help my peers navigate the digital world—from building professional online profiles to communicating safely and responsibly. This repository serves as my **Digital Literacy Portfolio**, documenting five key tasks that demonstrate foundational digital skills required for academic and professional success.

## 🛠️ Module Summaries

### **Module 1: Digital Literacy Awareness**

I created a visual infographic to help batchmates understand the definition of digital literacy and essential safe internet practices. This resource covers topics such as useful digital tools and professional online presence.

  * **Deliverable:** [Infographic Link](https://github.com/sujittstude-star/Digital-Literacy/blob/main/task-1-presentation/Task-1-Infographic.jpg)

### **Module 2: Professional Digital Portfolio**

I established a foundational professional presence on GitHub and LinkedIn. As a **Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics** student, this portfolio marks the beginning of my professional identity and my commitment to mastering digital investigation and security protocols.

* **Deliverable:** https://github.com/sujittstude-star/Digital-Literacy/tree/main/task-2-portfolio
### **Module 3: Coding & Collaboration**

I practiced technical problem-solving on HackerRank by completing beginner-level Python challenges. Additionally, I developed a digital literacy awareness quiz using Google Forms to facilitate peer learning and data organization.

  * **Deliverable:** [Digital Literacy Quiz Link](https://forms.gle/8uTPsVqrpqsbUe8VA)

### **Module 4: Professional Communication**

I drafted professional emails for academic extensions and internship inquiries to demonstrate effective digital communication. I also created a "Do's and Don'ts" guide for responsible social media conduct as a college student.

### **Module 5: Cybercrime Awareness**

I researched UPI and online payment fraud, producing a detailed case study and a prevention checklist. This resource includes specific tips for financial safety and information on reporting via the National Cyber Crime Portal.
