Social Media Checklist
The Do's
Professional Branding: Use a professional profile picture on platforms like LinkedIn to build a credible brand.
Information Verification: Verify information from multiple sources before sharing news or academic resources.
Respectful Networking: Network respectfully with alumni and industry professionals by sending personalized messages.
Privacy Management: Update your privacy settings regularly to control who can see your personal posts and photos.
Respect Intellectual Property: Credit original creators when sharing articles, code, or digital art.
The Don'ts
Negative Posting: Post offensive or discriminatory content that could negatively impact your future career.
Oversharing: Share sensitive personal details, such as your hostel room number, phone number, or government IDs.
Toxic Engagement: Engage with online trolls or participate in "flame wars" that can escalate into cyberbullying.
Unauthorized Tagging: Tag others in photos or videos without their explicit permission.
Unprofessional Communication: Use unprofessional language or slang when commenting on public academic or industry forums.
