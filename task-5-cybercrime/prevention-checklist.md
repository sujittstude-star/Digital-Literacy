Part B: Prevention Checklist

Stay Safe Online: A Guide for Students

   1. Never Share your UPI PIN: Remember, you do not need to enter your PIN to receive money, only to send it.

   2. Verify the Source: Before clicking any links regarding refunds or prizes, verify the sender’s identity through official channels

   3. Enable Biometric Locks: Use fingerprint or face ID locks for all payment apps to prevent unauthorized access if your phone is lost.

   4. Beware of Public Wi-Fi: Avoid conducting any financial transactions or logging into sensitive accounts while on unsecured public networks.

   5. Use Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Always enable 2FA on your email, social media, and banking apps.

   6. Check Permissions: Regularly review app permissions on your phone; if a simple calculator app asks for your contacts and location, delete it.

   7. Official Links Only: Only download apps from the official Google Play Store or Apple App Store.

   8. Stay Updated: Keep your phone’s operating system and security apps updated to protect against the latest malware.

How to Report:

National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in

Helpline Number: Dial 1930 (24x7) to report financial fraud immediately.
